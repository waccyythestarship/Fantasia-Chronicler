The gods are the ancestors of all life in the vast world. The Covenant of Light and the Faith of Solas mainly worship the new gods while the Disciples of Death and Prayers of the Abyss mainly worship the old gods.

# History
The Old Gods initially discovered a continent, that was later named Aurora by the new gods. Nyktos resided in the underworld and founded the great city of [[Noux Ulgoth]]. It is believed that the old gods inhabited the lands for roughly three thousand years. The gods created life during this time, mainly grotesque and twisted creatures, for entertainment, letting it evolve with time; many of which still exist, like the various species of dragons and great titans. 
\
\
The lands were desolate and engulfed in chaos for many years until the New Gods arrived. They first brought light, then the seas, and at last, created life with reason. The brilliant light warded off the corruption and made the lands habitable for crops and creatures. Thus, the Twilight Era began.

## Twilight Era
Aurora was beautiful. But it is missing life, something similar to their kind. So then it is decided that the gods will make descendants of their own. The demigods were brought into the world as the first intelligent life. They are born with some aspects from their forebear and are given the role to make more life in the lands and rule a prosperous civilization, which not all demigods followed. Mankind was born from Aedrys, and a close relative of the human race was born from Heddwyn. In another continent, the first elves were created by Ermira, from which they later evolved into other races of elves.
\
\
The first kingdoms were born around the latter half of the Twilight Era, such as the Reman Kingdom and Eithar. After a few hundred years, the Incursion of Nyktos occurred, otherwise known as the [[War of the Gods]], which lasted almost a year. It ended with the [[Battle of the Crown]], which led to the deaths of thousands, including Remos himself.

## First Era
The first era began merely half a day after the death of Remos. It starts when the sun takes its first dawn in the lands. The death of the god of light and the prophecy of Nyktos strike fear amongst the populace; civilizations are in disarray, Eithar forms a militaristic Empire to defend itself from foreign entities, and the demigods live in fear.

The death of Remos was followed by the death of Oohr, and it is shrouded in mystery, like how his tale has been to the gods. Only a few years after his death, the goddess of life perishes from an unknown cause; a giant tree they call the Great Eyrrneth Tree sprouted from where she was buried. Yearly, it produces a sap that can heal wounds and cure sickness. The demigods were left to their own devices.

Aedrys left the Reman Kingdom and lived in solitude on a remote island. 
\
\
Aneirin tried to rule after him, only for his people to revolt, wanting to remove the crown from the weaker demigod. Exiled from the lands, though the demigod was able to blend into society to observe the kingdom.
\
\
Emrys, after thousands of years looking after the garden of Caendell, decided to leave and guide caen elven groups across the continent of Ettrion.
\
\
Charia ventured to lay low and integrate into the mortal life, observing how they act. 
\
\
Firlia remained an apostle and roams the vast underworld.
\
\
Heddwyn led a clan of true-blooded Nordics and sailed north, in hopes of searching more land. 
\
\
Iris continued to travel the rest of the unknown world, wanting to see what is beyond Aurora.
\
\
Kassia looked after the Eyrrneth Tree and the rest of the garden when Emrys left Caendell.
