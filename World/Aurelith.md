Aurelith is one of the four continents in the known world, the others being Anthrim, Ettrion, and Dretorin. It is known to be the birthplace of mankind and the home of the new gods. As of the current day, it is covered by 11 nations, three of which are in an alliance known as the "Three Crowns." Though, while not officially recognized as nations, two dwarven colonies are established deep within the northern and southern mountain ranges, respectively.

# Geography
The continent of Aurelith is considered second-largest. It stretches for roughly 2,450 miles north to south and 2,300 miles east to west. It consists of 18 regions: [[Aurelith#Northwall|Northwall]], [[Aurelith#Gwyndaear|Gwyndaear]], [[Aurelith#Westmarch|Westmarch]], [[Aurelith#Dorene|Dorene]], [[Aurelith#Aurelle|Aurelle]], [[Aurelith#The Arvent|The Arvent]], [[Aurelith#The Rift|The Rift]], [[Aurelith#Serrado|Serrado]], [[Aurelith#Zajari|Zajari]], [[Aurelith#The Reach|The Reach]], [[Aurelith#Hagion|Hagion]], [[Aurelith#Crownlands|Crownlands]], [[Aurelith#Vyndfell|Vyndfell]], [[Aurelith#Northreach|Northreach]], [[Aurelith#Eastmarsh|Eastmarsh]], [[Aurelith#Osteria|Osteria]],  [[Aurelith#Mornval|Mornval]], and [[Aurelith#Cinderfall|Cinderfall]].

## Northwall
Northwall is the northernmost region of Aurelith, located north-west in Wintergard. Despite this, it is the second-most coldest region in the continent. It is generally covered by mountains, small forests and great snowy plains, many of which shelter draugrs and the frostbitten undead. Its climate is cold but calm, although it gets severely cold during winter. Because of these conditions, only a small number of Wintergard settlements are found within the region.

## Gwyndaear
Gwyndaear is the eastern region of the Aenglik Kingdoms. It comprises mainly the Gwynic Highlands and the thick forests. It is home to [[Silkshire]], widely considered the friendliest city in all of Aurelith. It is the coldest region in the Aenglik and is home to the Gwynic, the brave and tough warriors used in the Aenglik military. Moreover, it is a common dwelling for Sunbirds and Lesser Wyverns, and its wilderness can be swarming with outlaws.

## Westmarch
Westmarch is the western region of the Aenglik Kingdoms. It is primarily composed of low hills and plains, sparsely populated by forests, with the Eldmere River running through the south of the region and mountainous terrain to the north bordering the Province of Wintergard. It has a temperate climate with occasional rainfall and is home to diverse wildlife.

## Dorene
Dorene is the northern region of Solaria that borders Wintergard. It consists of mountainous terrain to the north and west, with tall dense forests and gently rolling hills in the rest of the region, and sparse wildlife, making it a suitable place for well-defended towns. Its mountains are rich in salt and iron and are considered the third richest region in all of Aurelith.

## Aurelle
Aurelle is the southern region of Solaria, covering three-fifths of the kingdom. It is the most temperate region in Fantasia, with pleasant weather year-round, and is considered the richest in all of Aurelith. It comprises mainly of great plains and occasional forests with the Goldwater River running through, and a large thicket to the east by the mountains. The region is rich in valuable materials, such as diamonds, iron, salt, and magical gems. It is well guarded by Solarian military, making the wilderness safe to travel, particularly due to the lack of bandits and beasts.
