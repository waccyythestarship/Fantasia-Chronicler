The gods are the ancestors of all life in the vast world. The Covenant of Light and the Faith of Solas mainly worship the new gods while the Disciples of Death and Prayers of the Abyss mainly worship the old gods.

---
[^1]: The Old Gods, Nyktos and Nyrva, initially found what is now the continent of Aurelith. Nyktos resided in the underworld of the continent and founded the great city of [[Noux Ulgoth]]. It is believed that the old gods inhabited the lands for three thousand years, roughly 3000 B.T. *(Before Twilight Era)* until 12 B.T. Many lifeforms were created and evolved during this time, such as the many species of dragons and the first humanoids.
\
\
The New Gods arrived by the year 12 B.T. With Remos bringing his light to the land. When light shines for the first time in the lands, Ermira brings life and Oohr makes the seas. Twelve years were spent cleansing the lands.
